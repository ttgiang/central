19. For course modifications involving content change as well as for new courses:

Ag 205, Agricultural Technologies

Objectives:

To learn the basic concepts associated with technologies applied to raw and processed agricultural products with landson laboratory exercises which provide students with product development experience.  Locally grown and underutilized commodities will be primarily used in product development.

Grading:  One midterm (25%), one final (25%), one sumitted final product (25%) with a written report (25%) outlining the major steps in product development.

Lecture Material:

Introduction to Agriculture (4 lectures)

Introduction to Agricultural Technologies (5 lectures)

Processing and manufacturing of agricultural products (5 lectures)

Product development and production (5 lectures)

Local and national case studies indlucing evlaution of success and failure factors (9 lectures)

Laboratory:

Two field trips to successful local agricultural businesses

Testing different technologies

Product evaluation

Researching new products

Developing new products

Showcasing new products
